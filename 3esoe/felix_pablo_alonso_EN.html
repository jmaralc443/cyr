His life

Fernando Alonso Díaz, born in Oviedo in 1981, showed a great passion for motorsport from a young age. His talent led him to rise quickly to Formula 1, where he became one of the most outstanding drivers thanks to his skill and determination. In addition to competing in other motorsport categories, he has spearheaded projects to train young drivers and has remained a key figure in international motorsport.

His career

Fernando Alonso's career began in karting, where he stood out from a young age for his talent and competitiveness. After gaining experience and recognition, he moved up through the categories until reaching Formula 1 in the early 2000s. Throughout his career, he has competed with several renowned teams, demonstrating his ability to adapt to different cars and situations. In addition to Formula 1, Alonso has participated in endurance racing and other motorsport disciplines, showcasing his versatility as a driver. His career is characterized by consistency, ambition, and a continuous pursuit of new challenges within the world of motorsport.

His current life

Fernando Alonso is fully engaged as an Aston Martin driver, with a firm commitment to his team, an eye on the future (beyond 2026), and the intention to compete at the highest level as long as he is motivated and able to do so. Despite his experience, he maintains a highly professional and ambitious attitude.
